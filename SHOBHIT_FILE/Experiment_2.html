Top 10 Tourist Places in India

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal in Agra is a symbol of love built by Shah Jahan. It is one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Jaipur

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is famous for its palaces, forts, and vibrant culture.

Kerala

Kerala is known for its backwaters, greenery, and peaceful houseboat experiences.

Goa

Goa is famous for its beaches, nightlife, and Portuguese heritage.

Varanasi

Varanasi is one of the oldest cities in the world, known for its ghats and spiritual atmosphere.

Ladakh

Ladakh is known for its mountains, monasteries, and breathtaking landscapes.

Shimla

Shimla is a popular hill station with scenic beauty and colonial architecture.

Darjeeling

Darjeeling is famous for tea gardens and views of the Kanchenjunga mountain.

Mysore

Mysore is known for its royal heritage and the magnificent Mysore Palace.

Amritsar

Amritsar is home to the Golden Temple, one of the most sacred places for Sikhs.
